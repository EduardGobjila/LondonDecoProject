About Us

Welcome to our team of painters and decorators, North London. For over 17 years, we have been providing North London residents and local businesses with top quality, fully insured and guaranteed painting and decorating services that have been always proudly delivered to their complete satisfaction. LondonDeco works closely with residential and commercial customers and clients mainly in the areas of Muswell Hill, Islington, Highgate, Highgate Village, Hampstead, Hampstead Heath, Golders Green, East Finchley, Crouch End, Alexander Park and North London. We are a team of fully-committed, quality-driven, passionate and friendly providing complete painting and decorating services at affordable prices. Our experience and expertise across the North London painting and decorating sector help us to understand our customers’ needs and requirements and find the best professional solution for everything and everyone. For your peace of mind, LondonDeco always maintains friendly, clean, tidy and safe working environment and warrants all its labour and products. We are proud to use only and always high quality and eco-friendly products. Thank you for spending time for visiting and reading about us and our services page.
